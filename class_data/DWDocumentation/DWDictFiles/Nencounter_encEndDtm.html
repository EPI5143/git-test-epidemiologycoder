The following comments provide additional information regarding the data element. These comments are meant to provide knowledge base of information gained through experience using the DW. The comments are maintained by the DW Support Team and collected over time from users of the DW as well as the DW Support Team as information is gathered.

2005/01/25

2005/01/25

Emergency encounters will not have an end date until they are closed, at which time the ER gone date will be used as an end date.

2005/01/25

2005/01/25

For SMS cases that can have multiple visits (such as Ambulatory Care encounters at the General), the end date will be the same as the start date, which is the visit date from the SMS visit segment.

2005/01/25

2005/01/25

For all other SMS encounters (except ER and multiple-visit encounters already described), the discharge date and time will be used as the end date and time. Note that active inpatients and open-ended outpatient encounters wont have an end date and time.

2005/09/30

2005/10/03

AmbCare visits at the General are created when a clinic is closed. Therefore, the visit creation date may be after the actual visit date. There are approx. 18 encounters with end dates prior to 1996 and 17 of these are from December 1995.
